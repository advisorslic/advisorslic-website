## What is the IC-38 Exam?

The **IC-38 exam** is a mandatory certification exam prescribed under the insurance regulatory framework for people who want to work as:
- **LIC Agent**
- **Bima Sakhi**
- **Life Insurance Advisor**

The goal is simple: ensure every advisor understands insurance fundamentals and follows ethical and compliant selling practices.

## What Type of Questions Come in IC-38?

The IC-38 exam is **concept-based** and focused on practical understanding, not tough mathematics.

### 1) Insurance Fundamentals
- Purpose of insurance  
- Risk and risk management  
- Principles of insurance  
**Example:** What is the main purpose of life insurance?

### 2) Life Insurance Concepts
- Types of policies (Term / Endowment / Money-back)  
- Premium, sum assured, maturity, nominee, claim basics

### 3) LIC Products Overview (Basics)
- Savings plans, protection plans, pension/annuity basics  
- Choosing the right plan based on customer needs

### 4) Role & Responsibilities of an LIC Agent / Bima Sakhi
- Advisor duties  
- Code of conduct  
- Customer-first approach and ethical selling

### 5) Regulations & IRDAI Basics
- Role of IRDAI  
- Basic compliance and rules  
- Transparency and disclosure
