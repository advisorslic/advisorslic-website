## Basic Eligibility Criteria

Before collecting documents, make sure you meet the basic eligibility:

- Minimum age: 18 years  
- Educational qualification: 10th  
- Indian citizen  
- Not blacklisted by any insurer  

Once eligible, prepare the following documents.

---

## Documents Required for LIC Agent Registration

### 1. Identity Proof

Any one valid government ID:

- Aadhaar Card  
- PAN Card  
- Voter ID  
- Passport  

Ensure name spelling matches across all documents.

---

### 2. Address Proof

You can use:

- Aadhaar Card  
- Electricity Bill  
- Rent Agreement  
- Passport  

For candidates in Ghaziabad, Noida or Delhi, address proof must be current and valid.

---

### 3. Educational Qualification Certificate

Minimum qualification certificate (as required by LIC norms).

Keep original and photocopy ready.

---

### 4. PAN Card (Mandatory)

PAN is compulsory for:

- Commission payment  
- Income tracking  
- Tax compliance  

Make sure PAN details are correct.

---

### 5. Passport Size Photographs

Recent color passport-size photographs with plain background.

Keep multiple copies ready.

---

### 6. Bank Account Details

You will need:

- Cancelled cheque  
- Bank account details  

Commission income is credited directly to your bank account.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Name mismatch in Aadhaar and PAN  
- Old or invalid address proof  
- Incorrect bank details  
- Missing signature  

Even small errors can delay approval.

---

## What Happens After Document Submission?

Once documents are verified:

1. Register for IC-38 exam  
2. Complete mandatory training  
3. Pass the IC-38 exam  
4. Receive license approval  

After that, you can officially start working as a LIC Agent in Delhi, Noida or Ghaziabad.
