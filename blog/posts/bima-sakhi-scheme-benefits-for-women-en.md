What Is the Bima Sakhi Scheme?
---
The Bima Sakhi Scheme is a women-centric initiative by LIC under which women are trained and appointed as LIC Agents (Insurance Advisors).

Under this scheme, selected women receive:
---
Professional training by LIC

Guidance for the IC-38 examination

Knowledge of LIC insurance, pension, and savings products

Official authorization to work as LIC advisors after qualifying

The term Bima Sakhi represents a woman who becomes a trusted insurance guide for families and communities.
