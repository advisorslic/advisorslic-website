# What Is the Bima Sakhi Scheme? Why It Is Important for Women and Its Key Benefits

Empowering women through financial independence and meaningful careers is a key focus in India today. Keeping this vision in mind, LIC (Life Insurance Corporation of India) introduced a special initiative known as the **Bima Sakhi Scheme**, designed exclusively for women.

This scheme provides women in Delhi, Noida and Ghaziabad with an opportunity to build a respected, flexible, and income-generating career as an LIC insurance advisor.

## What Is the Bima Sakhi Scheme?

The Bima Sakhi Scheme is a women-centric initiative by LIC under which women are trained and appointed as LIC Agents (Insurance Advisors).

Under this scheme, selected women receive:

- Professional training by LIC  
- Guidance for the IC-38 examination  
- Knowledge of LIC insurance, pension, and savings products  
- Official authorization to work as LIC advisors after qualifying  

The term *Bima Sakhi* represents a woman who becomes a trusted insurance guide for families and communities.

## Who Can Apply for the Bima Sakhi Scheme?

- Are 18 years or older  
- Meet basic educational requirements
