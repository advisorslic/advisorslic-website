---

## How Does an LIC Agent Earn?

An LIC agent earns income from three main sources:

### 1️⃣ First-Year Commission  
Earn approximately **25%–35% commission** on new policies sold.

### 2️⃣ Renewal Commission  
Every year when your client pays the premium, you receive **5%–7.5% renewal commission**.

### 3️⃣ Bonuses & Incentives  
Additional rewards for achieving performance targets.

👉 Work once, earn for years.

---

## Is LIC Agent Income Stable?

Yes — and this is the biggest strength of LIC agency.

If you build a strong client base in the first 2–3 years:

- You receive annual renewal income  
- Clients give referrals  
- Your network grows  
- Income increases gradually every year  

This is known as a **Recurring Income Model**.

## Who Can Become an LIC Agent?

- Minimum age: 18 years  
- Minimum qualification: 10th pass  
- Must pass the IC-38 exam  
- Complete basic training
