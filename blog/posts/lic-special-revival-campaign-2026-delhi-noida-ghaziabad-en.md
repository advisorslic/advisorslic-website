    <h2>What is LIC Special Revival Campaign?</h2>
    <p>
      The <strong>LIC Special Revival Campaign</strong> is a limited-period opportunity where eligible lapsed LIC policies
      can be revived by paying due premiums and meeting required conditions. In many cases, LIC may provide
      <strong>concession/relaxation on revival charges</strong> (subject to rules, plan type, policy status, and campaign guidelines).
    </p>

    <h2>Who Should Use This Campaign?</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Policyholders whose LIC policy has become <strong>lapsed</strong> due to missed premiums.</li>
      <li>People who want to <strong>continue life cover</strong> and keep their financial protection active.</li>
      <li>Families in <strong>Delhi, Noida, Ghaziabad</strong> searching for “LIC policy revival near me”.</li>
      <li>Those planning to restore benefits like <strong>maturity value</strong>, <strong>bonus (if applicable)</strong>, and policy continuity.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Key Benefits of Reviving a Lapsed LIC Policy</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Life cover continues</strong> and protection restarts as per policy terms.</li>
      <li>Helps protect your family’s future in case of unforeseen events.</li>
      <li>Restores policy continuity and helps avoid re-buying a new policy at older age.</li>
      <li>May help you save via <strong>concession on revival/late charges</strong> during campaign period (if eligible).</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>How to Revive LIC Policy in Delhi NCR (Delhi / Noida / Ghaziabad)</h2>
    <ol>
      <li><strong>Confirm policy status</strong> (lapsed/paid-up) and last premium due.</li>
      <li>Check if your policy is <strong>eligible</strong> under the Special Revival Campaign rules.</li>
      <li>Submit required documents and revival request (if needed).</li>
      <li>Pay due premiums and charges (with applicable concession, if any).</li>
      <li>Get confirmation of revival and keep the receipt/acknowledgement safely.</li>
    </ol>
