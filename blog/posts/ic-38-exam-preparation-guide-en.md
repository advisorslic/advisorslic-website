## What Is the IC-38 Exam?

The **IC-38 examination** is a mandatory certification exam prescribed by **IRDAI (Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India)** for individuals who wish to work as:

- LIC Agents  
- Bima Sakhi  
- Life Insurance Advisors  

The purpose of the IC-38 exam is to ensure that insurance advisors:
- Understand insurance fundamentals  
- Follow ethical sales practices  
- Provide correct and transparent information to customers

## FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

### How many questions are asked in the IC-38 exam?
The IC-38 exam consists of **50 multiple-choice questions (MCQs)**.

### What is the time limit for the IC-38 exam?
The total duration of the IC-38 exam is **60 minutes (1 hour)**.

### What are the passing marks for the IC-38 exam?
Candidates must score **35% marks**, which means **18 correct answers out of 50**.

### Is there negative marking in the IC-38 exam?
No, there is **no negative marking** in the IC-38 examination.

### What type of questions come in the IC-38 exam?
The exam includes **concept-based MCQs** related to insurance basics, life insurance products, agent responsibilities, ethics, and IRDAI regulations.

### Is the IC-38 exam difficult for beginners?
No, the IC-38 exam is considered **easy to moderate**, especially for beginners, homemakers, and Bima Sakhi applicants, if proper training is followed.

### Is IC-38 mandatory to become an LIC Agent or Bima Sakhi?
Yes, qualifying the **IC-38 exam is mandatory** to work as an LIC Agent or Bima Sakhi.

### How many attempts are allowed for the IC-38 exam?
There is **no fixed limit** on the number of attempts. Candidates can reappear if they do not pass.
