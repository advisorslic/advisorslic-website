Who can become an LIC Agent?

Any Indian citizen aged 18 years or above and minimum 10th pass can apply to become an LIC Agent.

How much income does an LIC Agent earn?

Income is primarily through commission and renewal income. Earnings depend on performance and consistency.

What is Bima Sakhi?

Bima Sakhi is a women-focused opportunity that can include stipend support (as per program) along with commission income.

Is LIC Agency part-time or full-time?

It can be part-time or full-time. You can work flexibly according to your convenience.

Is there any exam to become an LIC Agent?

Yes, after training you need to clear the IRDAI examination to proceed as per applicable guidelines.

How can I apply for LIC Agent or Bima Sakhi?

You can apply using the Apply Now form, WhatsApp, or by calling directly. Complete guidance is provided.
